Ground Beef Tacos on Soft Shell Tortillas

Ground Beef seasoned with taco seasoning, on a soft-shell tortilla, topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1lb)
  • Taco seasoning mix (2 packets) (You may use reduced sodium packets)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomato
  • 1/2 cup lettuce
  • Hot sauce to preference
  • 4-12 tortillas (depends on size of tortilla)

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium heat.
  2. Put ground beef in pan and break it up to cover the whole surface of the pan.
  3. Stir frequently. Allow to cook until completely browned, no pink left at all.
  4. Drain the grease into sink while running hot water.
  5. Stir in water and seasoning. Reduce heat to low-medium and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Put meat in tortilla. Scoop toppings on top with a spoon. Hot sauce to preference.
